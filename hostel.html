HOSTEL

Baba Ajai Singh boys hostel has been constructed with all the more turned architect techniques. It is spacious and well ventilated.the hostel is a seven storeyed building having 102 rooms facilitate it with cop boats, 24 hours electricity, water supply and internet facility. Apart from this the hostel has mess facility, first-aid room, guestroom, TV room and a common room.

HOSTEL'S MESS

Service in the mess shall be between scheduled hours only & closed after lunch on every Saturday for maintenance. The Mess timings are as follows and the students should strictly adhere to these timings. Working Days Breakfast 7.00 am to 8.45 am Lunch 1.15 pm to 2.00 pm Snacks 4.45 pm to 5.45 pm Dinner 7.00 pm to 9.00 pm The Self system of disposable will be followed in all messes. No food will be reserved for the late comers. sunday is a feast day, that day one sweet should be served.
