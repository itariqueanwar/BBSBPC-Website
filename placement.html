Training & Placement

The target of training & placement cell is to provide employment opportunities and world class training to BBSBPC students in the leading organizations. The cell is well equipped with complete infrastructure in terms of seminar hall, consultancy hall for pre-placement talks and presentations etc.

TRAINING

Our students are given first hand experience of hard core work of the industries by our fully technically sound labs and workshops. Many training programmes have been included in their academic routine so that they can be aware of the new technologies. Students also undergo a 4 weeks Industrial Training at the end of 4th semester as per their chosen stream and their performance is assessed for 50 marks by a panel of HODs and Lecturers besides experts from external agencies.

The Organization which the students of this college undergo training are M/s Punjab Tractors Limited (PTL) Mohali • DCW Patiala • Federal-Mogul, Goetze (India) Limited • G.S.Auto, Kartar Agro Industries • Bhusan Power & Steel Industries • Isgec Yamunanagar • Glaxo Smith Kline Health Care Ltd • Bhallarpur Industries Ltd • Net Max Chandigarh • NIIT Computer Centre Mohali • CAL-C Computer Centre • Aptech Computer Centre • Passar Bharti • ETDC Mohali • PunCom Mohali • A.I.R. Patiala • Dixon Electronics Mohali • BSNL

This training at the industry level also gives an overall view to the industry about the performance, technical knowledge and communication skills of our student. The students are also given live projects in their 6th semester as major projects which they have to present to external examiner. Not only to keep them busy with academics, students are given opportunities to participate in sports, cultural programs and Technical Exhibition at College as well as at Inter-Polytechnic level.

TRAINING AND PLACEMENT CELL Further, to achieve the trust reposed by student in the college management, the Training & Placement Cell works towards recognizing the competencies and performance of the students. As a prelude, the Training & Placement Cell has been interacting with the Industries for Industrial visits with the sole aim of accomplishing Institute-Industry relationship and goodwill. To further strengthen the Institute-Industry relationships, the college interact with the industries to identify their problems and give it to the students as their major project work. Further, to fine polish their technical skills and knowledge, eminent professionals are invited.

The Pass out Students of have been placed in various reputed industries. Quite a large number of students are pursuing their BE/ B.Tech courses.
